# AIRA Impact Model

## Problem Statement
Indian retail investors face fragmented information, delayed analysis, and inconsistent decision discipline. This creates a high risk of impulsive trades, avoidable losses, and low confidence in market participation.

## Target Users
- Active retail investors in Indian equities.
- First-time investors needing explainable guidance.
- Content and research teams needing fast daily market summarization.

## Impact Calculations

### 1) Time Saved
Assumptions:
- Manual daily workflow per user: 90 minutes.
- AIRA-assisted workflow: 25 minutes.
- Time saved per user/day: 65 minutes.

Math:
- Daily hours saved per user: 65 / 60 = 1.083 hours.
- For 10,000 active users/day: 10,000 x 1.083 = 10,830 hours/day.
- Annualized (250 market days): 10,830 x 250 = 2,707,500 hours/year.

### 2) Research Cost Reduction
Assumptions:
- Equivalent analyst cost baseline: INR 900/hour.
- Effective automation substitution: 30% of saved hours.

Math:
- Substituted hours/day: 10,830 x 0.30 = 3,249 hours/day.
- Cost saved/day: 3,249 x 900 = INR 2,924,100.
- Cost saved/year (250 days): INR 731,025,000.

### 3) Returns Improvement Potential
Assumptions:
- Better signal filtering + risk-aware summaries improve decision quality by 1.5% annualized for engaged users.
- Average deployable capital/user: INR 250,000.
- Engaged users: 10,000.

Math:
- Total capital base: 10,000 x 250,000 = INR 2,500,000,000.
- Incremental value at 1.5%: INR 37,500,000 annually.

## Revenue Opportunity for ET Markets
Potential monetization levers:
- Premium retail subscription for multi-agent analytics.
- Sponsored insights and contextual market intelligence modules.
- API licensing for broker and fintech partner integrations.

Illustrative model:
- 50,000 premium users x INR 149/month = INR 7,450,000/month.
- Annualized subscription revenue: INR 89,400,000.
- Additional B2B/API opportunities can layer on top.

## Assumptions
- Adoption ramps with product-market fit and trust.
- Data quality from external feeds remains stable.
- Users follow recommendations as decision support, not deterministic advice.
- Compliance/disclaimer UX remains visible in all conversational outputs.

## Conclusion
AIRA can materially reduce analysis time, lower effective research costs, and improve decision consistency for Indian retail investors, while creating a scalable premium product and platform revenue opportunity for ET Markets.
